## Design Context

### Users
Indian food lovers and catering clients in the Los Angeles / Canoga Park area. Used on phones and desktops when hungry or planning events. Context: browsing menus, placing orders, inquiring about catering packages. Goal: order confidently and quickly, or submit a catering inquiry.

### Brand Personality
Authentic, premium, bold. Three words: warm, bold, celebratory. The brand feels like a high-end Indian catering house — not a corner takeout spot, not a sterile corporate restaurant chain. It should feel like the food smells when you walk in.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark luxury theme: deep warm browns and blacks, saffron orange (#E87000) as the hero accent, burgundy (#8B1A1A) as depth color, cream (#F5E6C8) for text. References: the warmth of Chipotle's brand, the ordering UX of Domino's, the premium feel of a fine dining website. Anti-reference: generic Indian restaurant site with red/yellow stock photography.

### Design Principles
1. **Warmth over polish** — every surface should feel inviting, not corporate
2. **Food first** — the menu is the hero; everything else supports it
3. **Trust through clarity** — checkout, cart, payment must feel clean and trustworthy
4. **Mobile-first ordering** — most customers order on phone; touch targets must be generous
5. **Consistent centering** — all hero content must be centered; no JS should shift layout elements off-axis
